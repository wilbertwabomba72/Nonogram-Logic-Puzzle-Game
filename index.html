How to Play

Simple to learn, satisfying to master.

Basic Rules

  • Each number tells you how many filled cells appear together in that row or column.
  • Multiple numbers mean separate groups, with at least one empty space between them.
  • Use the brush to fill correct cells.
  • Use the X tool to mark cells that should stay empty.
  • Finish the full pattern to clear the level.

Useful Tools

  • Brush: fill cells you believe are correct.
  • X: mark impossible cells.
  • Erase: remove a mistake.
  • Undo: step back quickly.
  • Hint: reveal a helpful move when needed.
