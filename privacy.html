Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nonogram: Logic Puzzle Game ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Game.

1. Overview

Nonogram: Logic Puzzle Game is a single-player puzzle game. The Game is designed for offline play, but some features related to advertising and technical operations may require internet access.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The Game may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

  • level progress
  • stars or completion status
  • audio and gameplay settings

This local data is used to preserve your game experience and is not required to create an account.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The Game includes third-party advertising technologies. These services may request ad configuration, load advertisements, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve ad delivery.

Depending on your device settings, region, and permissions, third-party advertising partners may process information such as:

  • device identifiers
  • advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • basic device and app information
  • diagnostic or performance data related to ad delivery

4. App Tracking Transparency

On Apple devices, the Game may display a permission request under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can choose whether to allow tracking. If you do not allow tracking, the Game may still display ads, but advertising functionality may be less personalized depending on third-party policies.

5. No Account Registration

The Game does not require you to create a user account, submit a username, or provide a password in order to play.

6. Children's Privacy

If the Game is made available to children in your region, parents or guardians should review how device permissions and advertising settings are managed. If you believe a privacy issue affects a child, please contact us and we will review the matter promptly.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce risk in the normal operation of the Game. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. International Use

If you use the Game outside your home country or region, third-party service providers may process data according to their own infrastructure and policies.

9. Your Choices

  • You can control tracking permission through your iOS settings.
  • You can delete the Game to remove local gameplay data stored by the app.
  • You can manage network access and ad-related permissions through device settings where available.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
